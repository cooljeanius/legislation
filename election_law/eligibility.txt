0. Relative to qualifications to appear on New Hampshire's Presidential Primary
   ballot
1. Upon receipt of a candidate's affirmation of eligibility to appear on New
   Hampshire's Presidential Primary ballot, the New Hampshire Secretary of State
   shall inquire into the accuracy of the candidate's provided information. Should
   the candidate's information be inaccurate in such a way that causes them to be
   ineligible for office, that candidate shall not be listed on New Hampshire's
   Presidential Primary ballot.
