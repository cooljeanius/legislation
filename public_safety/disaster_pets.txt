0. Relative to the safety of pets during emergency declarations
1. Whenever the governor issues an emergency declaration that includes an
evacuation order, any hotels in areas to which people are to be evacuated, which
are being required to shelter evacuated persons, shall also be required to shelter
any pets belonging to those evacuated persons.
