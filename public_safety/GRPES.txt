0. Relative to the use of explosives at gender reveal parties
1. Definitions
For the purposes of this legislation, a "gender reveal party" shall be defined as
an event in which people gather to learn the projected gender of an unborn child.
2. The use of explosives at gender reveal parties in the State of New Hampshire
is hereby prohibited.
