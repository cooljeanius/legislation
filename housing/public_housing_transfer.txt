0. Relative to interoperability of public housing data
1. All state-funded public housing programs shall store any data they keep about
   their residents in a manner that allows residents to transfer easily from one
   public housing project to another (even across state lines), should they chose
   to do so.

This became LSR 2023-0184, which then became HB355: https://gencourt.state.nh.us/lsr_search/billText.aspx?id=184&type=4
