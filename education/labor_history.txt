0. Relative to the teaching of labor history
1. The New Hampshire Department of Education shall require that New Hampshire
   schools teach labor history as part of their history curriculum. This shall
   include labor history of both New Hampshire and the US as a whole, including
   notable strikes, unionization drives, and other labor actions.
2. Curriculum chosen to fulfill the provisions of this act shall be chosen in a
   free and open manner, with decisions being made in collaboration with members
   of the labor movement.

(This became LSR 2022-2259)
