0. Relative to police unions
1. All labor unions representing police officers must perform solidarity
   strikes with any striking workers represented by any other officially
   recognized labor union within the jurisdiction of those police officers.
2. Any police union that fails to perform such a solidarity strike shall lose
   its right to organize.
2. Any individual police officer failing to walk the picket line alongside the
   other striking workers in such a strike shall lose his or her job.
